Frequently asked questions

How are tokens counted?

Tokens are roughly 4 characters or 0.75 words in English. This tool uses a close approximation of OpenAI's tokenizer — actual counts may vary by ±5%.

Does this work for Claude and Gemini?

Yes. Claude and Gemini use similar tokenization to GPT models for English text. For non-English text, token counts can differ significantly.

Is my text stored anywhere?

No. All processing happens in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
