Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This privacy policy explains how AI Prompt Token Counter ("we," "our," or "the Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website.

1. Data we collect

Text you paste into the tool: Your input text is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We do not send your text to any server, store it, log it, or transmit it over the internet. No data about the content of your prompts is collected by us.

Automatically collected information: When you visit the Service, your browser may send us standard HTTP headers including:

  • Your IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring page (if you clicked a link to reach us)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page

This information is collected by Google Analytics (see section 3 below) and Google AdSense (see section 4 below).

Cookies: Google AdSense and Google Analytics place cookies on your device to track your browsing behavior across websites. See section 4 for details.

2. How we use your data

We do not use personal data to contact you, sell to third parties, or create marketing profiles. Your data is used only for:

  • Understanding how many people use the Service and from where
  • Improving tool performance and user experience
  • Displaying relevant ads (handled by Google AdSense)

3. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand usage patterns. Google Analytics collects anonymised data such as page views, session duration, and referral source. This data helps us see which tools are popular and which keywords drive traffic.

Your privacy: Google Analytics does not collect your name, email, or personally identifying information. IP addresses are anonymised (the last octet is removed). Google does not sell this data to third parties.

Opt-out: You can disable Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Extension.

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Google AdSense

We monetise the Service using Google AdSense. AdSense serves ads based on your browsing history across websites you've visited (not your token counter input).

How it works: Google AdSense uses cookies and advertising identifiers to:

  • Track which websites you visit
  • Show you ads relevant to your interests
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraud

Your privacy: Google does not share your individual browsing data with us. We see only anonymised statistics like total impressions and clicks.

Opt-out: You can control personalised ads by:

  • Visiting Google's Ad Settings and toggling "Personalised ads" off
  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Extension
  • Using a privacy-focused browser like Brave or installing an ad blocker (we're fine with this)

Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Third-party links and content

Our Service may link to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Always review the privacy policy of any third-party site before providing information to it.

6. Data retention

We retain no persistent data on our servers. Your browser may store cookies from Google Analytics and AdSense — these typically expire after 13 months of inactivity. You can clear cookies from your browser settings at any time.

7. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. If you are under 13, do not use this Service. If we learn that a child under 13 has provided information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Security

The Service is served over HTTPS, which encrypts data in transit. Since we do not store your input text or personal data, there is minimal risk of data breach. However, no online service is 100% secure — use at your own risk.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes take effect when posted to this page. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitute your acceptance of the updated policy.
